Band 'The Tropics' is Back in Town

Our town's world famous band 'The Tropics' is back in town! After their latest world tour for their new album 'The Van' they have finally returned to their hometown of Sheffield, UK. 'The Van' was actually the band's first album in almost four years. They were reported to have returned Sunday night as they were spotted at a local bar.

The rumor around town is that the album was created by the lead singer, Alec Burner, after his latest heartbreak. Burner was reportedly seeing a girl by the name of Elise Richard who lives in a nearby city. They were seen together a number of times last summer, and it seems like the heartbreak was the inspiration for the new album.

Honorable Mentions in the Bookish Community

Destiny Sidwell

Fairly known in the 'BookTube' community with about 210k subscribers, she does a lot of reading challenges and spreads positivity whenever she can. She likes to give very honest reviews on books she reads so that people know a bit more about the books.



Haley Pham

A huge figure in the 'BookTube' community with almost 3 million people subscribed to her channel. A christian who owns her own small, private library. She reads over 100 books every year and likes to give a lot of good recommendations for people who are not sure what they should read next or where to start. She can brighten anyone's day by just posting a new video.
